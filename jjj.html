Culture

The Ivatans, known for their hospitality and resilience, are the locals of Batanes. They speak Ivatan, with Tagalog and English also widely used. Their unique stone houses reflect their adaptation to strong winds and typhoons.

Climate

Batanes has a tropical climate all year round. The best time to visit is from November to May during the dry season when the weather is cool and calm. Rainy months last from June to October with occasional typhoons.
